COMAC

COMAC (Commercial Aircraft Corporation of China) – China’s state-owned aerospace corporation, founded in 2008 and headquartered in Shanghai, aims to reduce dependence on Western corporations by developing domestic commercial aircraft. Notable aircraft lines:
* ARJ21: Regional aircraft, carrying 78-90 passengers, range 2,225-3,700 km, put into operation in 2016.
* C919: Narrow-body aircraft, carrying 156-192 passengers, range 4,075-5,555 km, starting operation in 2023 with China Eastern Airlines.
* CR929: Wide-body aircraft in cooperation with Russia, expected to carry 250-320 passengers, under development. COMAC represents China’s great ambition in the global commercial aviation industry.
